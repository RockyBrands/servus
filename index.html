Our Story

Since it's founding in 1922 in Rock Island, Illinois, Servus has specialized in offering a wide array of waterproof PVC and neoprene.

Servus offers an extensive line of footwear for general outdoors, landscaping, gardening, hunting, farming, occupational, processing and general industrial. Whether you find yourself on a construction site or working the nation's farmland, Servus footwear will keep you dry, warm, and comfortable.

Viral Penetration Resistance

Our Viral Penetration Resistant Boots have passed the Standard Test Method for Resistance of Materials Used in Protective Clothing to Penetration by Blood-Borne Pathogens using Phi-X174 Bacteriophage Penetration as a Test System ASTM F1671 / F1671M - 13.
